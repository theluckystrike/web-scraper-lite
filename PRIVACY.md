# Privacy Policy - Web Scraper Lite

**Effective Date:** February 16, 2026

## Overview

Web Scraper Lite is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension operates 100% locally on your device and **collects zero data**.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data.** Period.

## What This Extension Does

### Does NOT

- Send any data to external servers
- Track your browsing history or activity
- Collect usage analytics or telemetry
- Store data on remote servers
- Share data with third parties
- Use cookies or tracking mechanisms
- Execute remote code
- Access data from other tabs or websites

### Does

- Store your preferences locally using `chrome.storage.local` (saved only on your device)
- Access the content of the active tab when you use the extraction feature
- Write to clipboard when you copy extracted data

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Purpose | Data Access |
|------------|---------|-------------|
| `activeTab` | Extract data from the current webpage | Only when you activate the extension |
| `storage` | Save your selectors and settings | Stored locally on your device only |
| `clipboardWrite` | Copy extracted data | Only when you use the copy function |

## Local Storage

All extension data (your saved selectors, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the extension

## Third-Party Services

**Web Scraper Lite does not use any third-party services.** All functionality runs entirely within your browser.
